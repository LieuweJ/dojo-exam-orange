# Welcome to Orange-in-a-Row 🍊

Welcome to **Orange-in-a-Row** — clearly superior to **Connect Four**,
because it has **AI** *and* **Bluetooth**.

Orange-in-a-Row is a two-player strategy game where planning ahead is the key to victory.

Before we begin, here are the rules you need to know.

---

## The Board

- The board consists of **6 rows** and **7 columns**
- Columns are numbered **1 to 7**, from **left to right**

---

## How to Play

- Players take turns dropping a coin into a column
- On your turn, select a column by entering a number **1–7**
- The coin will **fall to the lowest available position** in that column
- **Player 1 always goes first**
- After that, players **alternate turns**

---

## Winning the Game

You win the game by placing **4 of your coins in a row**:

- Horizontally
- Vertically
- Diagonally

If all **42 positions** on the board are filled and no player has won, the game ends in a **draw**.
